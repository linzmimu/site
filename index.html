About Me

Hello!

I'm a world traveler, a knowledge seeker, and a health & wellness-obsessed yogi. As I've hopped around the globe in the past 5+ years, I've had the opportunity to experience such a variety of cultures, lifestyles, ideas, and careers.

Starting out in the physical sciences, I majored in Biology at the George Washington University, graduated with honors, and worked in biotechnology before leaving for Asia. I ended up in Taiwan, working in publishing as a writer and editor of a print magazine for the #1 company in the country for English education. I also became a co-host for the company's daily radio talk show.

I decided to move to Ubud, Bali and fell in love with the local culture: with yoga, health & wellness among the expats, and the beautiful and warm-hearted spiritual nature of the local Balinese people. I worked on a freelance basis for entrepreneurs with heart-centered businesses, providing technical assistance with e-commerce sites, podcasts, social media, in addition to general business administration.

I'm now back to the States – this time on the West coast – in the San Francisco Bay area.
